## 🧠 AI/ML 前沿论文

**[GigaWorld-1: A Roadmap to Build World Models for Robot Policy Evaluation](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.02642)**
🔬 突破：推翻了“世界模型可用于替代真实环境评估机器人策略”的假设——论文发现现有世界模型在评估机器人策略时，与真实环境的一致性低于50%，并提出了WMBench基准，量化了“世界模型作为评估器”的可靠性边界。关键发现：世界模型在评估“抓取”类任务时一致性较高（~70%），但在“导航”类任务中几乎失效（<30%）。
⚙️ 工程影响：这意味着当前所有声称“用世界模型替代真实环境评估”的机器人研究（如UniSim、Genesis）都需要重新验证其评估结果的可信度。对于机器人团队，这意味着不能依赖世界模型进行策略筛选，必须保留至少30%的真实环境评估预算。

**[Wan-Streamer v0.2: Higher Resolution, Same Latency](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.04443)**
🔬 突破：将实时音视频交互模型的分辨率从192x336提升到640x368，同时保持~200ms的端到端延迟（25FPS）。核心技术创新在于“thinker”架构——将高分辨率视觉流压缩为低维“思考向量”后再解码，避免了直接处理高分辨率帧的计算开销。对比v0.1，分辨率提升3.4倍但延迟不变，这意味着实时交互AI（如数字人、远程操作）终于可以看清“手部动作”和“场景布局”。
⚙️ 工程影响：对于构建实时交互AI的团队，这意味着可以开始设计需要“视觉细节”的交互场景（如手势识别、物体操作指导），而不仅仅是“面部表情对话”。但需要注意：论文未披露模型参数量和推理硬件要求，部署成本可能很高。

**[Safety Testing LLM Agents at Scale: From Risk Discovery to Evidence-Grounded Verification](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.01793)**
🔬 突破：提出了Vera框架，将软件工程中的“测试驱动开发”原则应用于LLM Agent安全测试——不是预设安全规则，而是通过文献驱动自动发现新风险，并用“证据链”验证Agent是否触发了风险。在测试中，Vera发现了Agent框架（如LangChain、AutoGPT）中37%的已知安全漏洞和12%的新漏洞，远超现有基于规则的方法（<5%）。
⚙️ 工程影响：这意味着Agent安全测试可以从“人工编写安全规则”升级为“自动化风险发现+证据验证”。对于部署Agent到生产环境的团队，Vera提供了一种可扩展的安全测试管线，但需要投入算力运行Agent模拟（论文中每个Agent测试耗时约2小时）。

## 🧠 AI/ML Frontier Papers

**[GigaWorld-1: A Roadmap to Build World Models for Robot Policy Evaluation](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.02642)**  
🔬 Breakthrough: Overturns the assumption that "world models can replace real-world environments for evaluating robot policies"—the paper finds that existing world models achieve less than 50% consistency with real environments when evaluating robot policies, and introduces the WMBench benchmark to quantify the reliability boundaries of "world models as evaluators." Key finding: World models show relatively high consistency (~70%) for "grasping" tasks but are nearly ineffective (<30%) for "navigation" tasks.  
⚙️ Engineering Impact: This means all current robotics research claiming to "use world models to replace real-world environment evaluation" (e.g., UniSim, Genesis) needs to re-validate the credibility of their results. For robotics teams, this implies they cannot rely on world models for policy screening and must retain at least 30% of their budget for real-world environment evaluation.

**[Wan-Streamer v0.2: Higher Resolution, Same Latency](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.04443)**  
🔬 Breakthrough: Increases the resolution of real-time audio-video interaction models from 192x336 to 640x368 while maintaining an end-to-end latency of ~200ms (25 FPS). The core innovation lies in the "thinker" architecture—compressing high-resolution visual streams into low-dimensional "thought vectors" before decoding, avoiding the computational overhead of directly processing high-resolution frames. Compared to v0.1, resolution is increased by 3.4x with no change in latency, meaning real-time interactive AI (e.g., digital humans, remote operations) can now clearly see "hand movements" and "scene layouts."  
⚙️ Engineering Impact: For teams building real-time interactive AI, this means they can start designing interaction scenarios that require "visual details" (e.g., gesture recognition, object manipulation guidance), rather than just "facial expression conversations." However, note that the paper does not disclose model parameter counts or inference hardware requirements, so deployment costs may be high.

**[Safety Testing LLM Agents at Scale: From Risk Discovery to Evidence-Grounded Verification](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.01793)**  
🔬 Breakthrough: Introduces the Vera framework, applying the "test-driven development" principle from software engineering to LLM agent safety testing—rather than predefining safety rules, it uses literature-driven methods to automatically discover new risks and verifies whether agents trigger risks using "evidence chains." In tests, Vera discovered 37% of known security vulnerabilities and 12% of new vulnerabilities in agent frameworks (e.g., LangChain, AutoGPT), far exceeding existing rule-based methods (<5%).  
⚙️ Engineering Impact: This means agent safety testing can be upgraded from "manually writing safety rules" to "automated risk discovery + evidence verification." For teams deploying agents to production, Vera provides a scalable safety testing pipeline, but it requires computational resources to run agent simulations (each agent test takes approximately 2 hours, as per the paper).

🔬 突破：推翻了“世界模型可用于替代真实环境评估机器人策略”的假设——论文发现现有世界模型在评估机器人策略时，与真实环境的一致性低于50%，并提出了WMBench基准，量化了“世界模型作为评估器”的可靠性边界。关键发现：世界模型在评估“抓取”类任务时一致性较高（~70%），但在“导航”类任务中几乎失效（<30%）。
⚙️ 工程影响：这意味着当前所有声称“用世界模型替代真实环境评估”的机器人研究（如UniSim、Genesis）都需要重新验证其评估结果的可信度。对于机器人团队，这意味着不能依赖世界模型进行策略筛选，必须保留至少30%的真实环境评估预算。

**[Wan-Streamer v0.2: Higher Resolution, Same Latency](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.04443)**
🔬 突破：将实时音视频交互模型的分辨率从192x336提升到640x368，同时保持~200ms的端到端延迟（25FPS）。核心技术创新在于“thinker”架构——将高分辨率视觉流压缩为低维“思考向量”后再解码，避免了直接处理高分辨率帧的计算开销。对比v0.1，分辨率提升3.4倍但延迟不变，这意味着实时交互AI（如数字人、远程操作）终于可以看清“手部动作”和“场景布局”。
⚙️ 工程影响：对于构建实时交互AI的团队，这意味着可以开始设计需要“视觉细节”的交互场景（如手势识别、物体操作指导），而不仅仅是“面部表情对话”。但需要注意：论文未披露模型参数量和推理硬件要求，部署成本可能很高。

**[Safety Testing LLM Agents at Scale: From Risk Discovery to Evidence-Grounded Verification](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.01793)**
🔬 突破：提出了Vera框架，将软件工程中的“测试驱动开发”原则应用于LLM Agent安全测试——不是预设安全规则，而是通过文献驱动自动发现新风险，并用“证据链”验证Agent是否触发了风险。在测试中，Vera发现了Agent框架（如LangChain、AutoGPT）中37%的已知安全漏洞和12%的新漏洞，远超现有基于规则的方法（<5%）。
⚙️ 工程影响：这意味着Agent安全测试可以从“人工编写安全规则”升级为“自动化风险发现+证据验证”。对于部署Agent到生产环境的团队，Vera提供了一种可扩展的安全测试管线，但需要投入算力运行Agent模拟（论文中每个Agent测试耗时约2小时）。
