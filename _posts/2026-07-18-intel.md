## 🧠 AI/ML 前沿论文

**[Token Time Continuous Diffusion for Language Modeling](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.14106)**
🔬 突破：推翻了“扩散语言模型必须所有token同时去噪”的假设。TTCD引入**per-token时间步**，让不同token以不同速度从噪声过渡到文本——关键token（如动词）加速，修饰词（如形容词）减速。在LM1B基准上，TTCD以**相同推理预算**比离散扩散模型（如MDLM）perplexity降低15%。
⚙️ 工程影响：per-token时间步意味着**推理时可以选择性跳过“不重要”token的去噪步骤**，将生成延迟从“序列长度×步数”降低到“关键token数×步数”。对于需要低延迟的实时文本生成（如对话Agent），TTCD可能比自回归模型更具竞争力——但代价是训练时需要预测每个token的“重要性权重”，增加了训练复杂度。

**[Hierarchical Denoising For Multi-Step Visual Reasoning](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.15278)**
🔬 突破：改进了“视频扩散模型只能生成，不能推理”的现状。HDR提出**层次化潜在空间**——顶层（语义层）做全局推理（如“物体A移动到B后面”），底层（像素层）做细节生成。在CLEVRER基准上，HDR的多步推理准确率比Sora（直接生成视频）高22%，推理延迟降低40%。
⚙️ 工程影响：层次化架构意味着**推理和生成可以共享同一个潜在空间**，不再需要“先推理再生成”的两阶段管线。对于机器人“感知→规划→执行”流程，HDR可能实现“边推理边生成”的实时闭环——但当前实现依赖CLEVRER的合成场景，真实场景的泛化性存疑。

**[RxBrain: Embodied Cognition Foundation Model with Joint Language-Visual Reasoning and Imagination](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.14187)**
🔬 突破：推翻了“具身智能需要分开训练语言模型和视觉模型”的假设。RxBrain将**语言和视觉想象编码到同一个“规划序列”**——语言提供抽象结构（“先拿杯子，再倒水”），视觉想象提供具体状态（“杯子在桌子的左上角”）。在EmbodiedQA基准上，RxBrain的任务成功率比RT-2（纯视觉模型）高18%，比PaLM-E（语言+视觉拼接）高11%。
⚙️ 工程影响：联合规划序列意味着**机器人可以“想象”任务执行过程后再行动**，避免“边做边想”导致的物理碰撞。对于仓库机器人、家庭服务机器人等场景，RxBrain可能将“试错成本”降低一个数量级——但当前模型参数量（7B）对边缘部署仍有挑战。

## 🧠 AI/ML Frontier Papers

**[Token Time Continuous Diffusion for Language Modeling](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.14106)**  
🔬 Breakthrough: Overturns the assumption that "diffusion language models must denoise all tokens simultaneously". TTCD introduces **per-token time steps**, allowing different tokens to transition from noise to text at different speeds—key tokens (like verbs) accelerate, while modifiers (like adjectives) decelerate. On the LM1B benchmark, TTCD achieves **15% lower perplexity** than discrete diffusion models (like MDLM) with the **same inference budget**.  
⚙️ Engineering Impact: Per-token time steps mean **inference can selectively skip denoising steps for "unimportant" tokens**, reducing generation latency from "sequence length × steps" to "key token count × steps". For real-time text generation requiring low latency (e.g., dialogue Agents), TTCD may be more competitive than autoregressive models—but at the cost of needing to predict each token's "importance weight" during training, increasing training complexity.

**[Hierarchical Denoising For Multi-Step Visual Reasoning](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.15278)**  
🔬 Breakthrough: Improves the current state where "video diffusion models can only generate, not reason". HDR proposes a **hierarchical latent space**—the top layer (semantic layer) handles global reasoning (e.g., "object A moves behind B"), while the bottom layer (pixel layer) handles detail generation. On the CLEVRER benchmark, HDR's multi-step reasoning accuracy is 22% higher than Sora (direct video generation), with 40% lower reasoning latency.  
⚙️ Engineering Impact: The hierarchical architecture means **reasoning and generation can share the same latent space**, eliminating the need for a two-stage pipeline of "reason first, then generate". For robot "perception → planning → execution" workflows, HDR may enable a real-time closed loop of "reasoning while generating"—but the current implementation relies on CLEVRER's synthetic scenes, and generalization to real-world scenarios is questionable.

**[RxBrain: Embodied Cognition Foundation Model with Joint Language-Visual Reasoning and Imagination](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.14187)**  
🔬 Breakthrough: Overturns the assumption that "embodied intelligence requires separate training of language and vision models". RxBrain encodes **language and visual imagination into the same "planning sequence"**—language provides abstract structure ("first grab the cup, then pour water"), while visual imagination provides concrete states ("the cup is at the top-left corner of the table"). On the EmbodiedQA benchmark, RxBrain's task success rate is 18% higher than RT-2 (pure vision model) and 11% higher than PaLM-E (language + vision concatenation).  
⚙️ Engineering Impact: The joint planning sequence means **robots can "imagine" the task execution process before acting**, avoiding physical collisions caused by "thinking while doing". For scenarios like warehouse robots and home service robots, RxBrain may reduce "trial-and-error costs" by an order of magnitude—but the current model size (7B parameters) still poses challenges for edge deployment.

## 🧠 AI/ML 前沿論文

**[Token Time Continuous Diffusion for Language Modeling](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.14106)**
🔬 突破：推翻了「擴散語言模型必須所有token同時去噪」的假設。TTCD引入**per-token時間步**，讓不同token以不同速度從雜訊過渡到文本——關鍵token（如動詞）加速，修飾詞（如形容詞）減速。在LM1B基準上，TTCD以**相同推理預算**比離散擴散模型（如MDLM）perplexity降低15%。
⚙️ 工程影響：per-token時間步意味著**推理時可以選擇性跳過「不重要」token的去噪步驟**，將生成延遲從「序列長度×步數」降低到「關鍵token數×步數」。對於需要低延遲的即時文本生成（如對話Agent），TTCD可能比自回歸模型更具競爭力——但代價是訓練時需要預測每個token的「重要性權重」，增加了訓練複雜度。

**[Hierarchical Denoising For Multi-Step Visual Reasoning](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.15278)**
🔬 突破：改進了「影片擴散模型只能生成，不能推理」的現狀。HDR提出**層次化潛在空間**——頂層（語義層）做全域推理（如「物體A移動到B後面」），底層（像素層）做細節生成。在CLEVRER基準上，HDR的多步推理準確率比Sora（直接生成影片）高22%，推理延遲降低40%。
⚙️ 工程影響：層次化架構意味著**推理和生成可以共享同一個潛在空間**，不再需要「先推理再生成」的兩階段管線。對於機器人「感知→規劃→執行」流程，HDR可能實現「邊推理邊生成」的即時閉環——但當前實現依賴CLEVRER的合成場景，真實場景的泛化性存疑。

**[RxBrain: Embodied Cognition Foundation Model with Joint Language-Visual Reasoning and Imagination](https://huggingface.co/papers/2607.14187)**
🔬 突破：推翻了「具身智能需要分開訓練語言模型和視覺模型」的假設。RxBrain將**語言和視覺想像編碼到同一個「規劃序列」**——語言提供抽象結構（「先拿杯子，再倒水」），視覺想像提供具體狀態（「杯子在桌子的左上角」）。在EmbodiedQA基準上，RxBrain的任務成功率比RT-2（純視覺模型）高18%，比PaLM-E（語言+視覺拼接）高11%。
⚙️ 工程影響：聯合規劃序列意味著**機器人可以「想像」任務執行過程後再行動**，避免「邊做邊想」導致的物理碰撞。對於倉庫機器人、家庭服務機器人等場景，RxBrain可能將「試錯成本」降低一個數量級——但當前模型參數量（7B）對邊緣部署仍有挑戰。
